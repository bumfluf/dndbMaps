How to Use

  1. Create a Google Drive folder for your campaign maps
  2. Share the folder with your Google account, set folder permissions to "Anyone with the link can view" or "Anyone with the link can edit" depending on your preference
  3. Copy the folder link from the address bar or share button
  4. Add your character using the form above:
    • Enter your character name (as it appears in D&D Beyond)
    • Paste the Google Drive folder link
    • Click "Test Connection" to verify access
    • Click "Add Character" to save
  5. View maps on D&D Beyond: When you view your character's sheet, click the "Maps" tab to see maps from the linked folder

Notes

  • Maps are loaded automatically when you switch to a character with a saved Google Drive folder
  • You can map multiple characters to different folders
  • Maps are cached in the browser for faster loading
  • PNG, JPEG, JPG, GIF, WEBP, SVG, and BMP images are supported
